I graduated from Lake Fenton High School in 2014. The cafeteria food was actually really good forbeing as unexpensive as it was. a full meal costed about 35 NOK and it included an entree, 1 or 2 sides and a drink. An example of one of the hot meals that was prepared had baked turkey and gravy as the entree, mashed potatoes and green beans as a side, and your choice of one of their milk or juice options. Water was always free.
